About Me

Hi there, may you have a nice day I'm Abdul Matin, a passionate UI/UX Designer who loves and enjoy creating user friendly experiences.

As a UI/UX Designer, Graphic Designer, and Frontend Developer, I've had the wonderful opportunity to grow both professionally and personally during my 2 years and 1 month of experience at a startup company. My journey began with a bachelor's degree in Information Technology, majoring in Database Systems, from Mindanao State University Main Campus.

Throughout my career, I've been fortunate to work with diverse teams and clients, each experience teaching me the value of collaboration and open-mindedness. Every project is an opportunity to learn, and I approach each one with humility, knowing that growth comes from embracing challenges.

While I've achieved certain milestones, I am humbly aware that there is always more to learn and explore. I embrace new challenges with enthusiasm, always striving to expand my horizons and become a better designer and developer.
